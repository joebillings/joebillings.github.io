Hello and welcome to Guppy Books, a newly-created publisher of books for children of all ages.

Having worked in children’s publishing for over twenty years, I am passionate about books for young people. The titles on the Guppy list will celebrate all genres – from younger middle-grade fiction to thought-provoking, mind-blowing novels for older readers. We want to produce the highest-quality and most entertaining books for young readers, originating from the UK and around the world.

Small and responsive, inclusive and communicative, the Guppy list will be supported by the very best freelancers in publicity, sales, marketing and design. As publishing director at David Fickling Books, I worked on many bestselling and award-winning books, and curating my own list of books is a dream come true.

Books have the power to open minds and convey universal stories, something that has always been essential but is perhaps more important than ever at this moment in time. With a number of titles already acquired from some incredible authors and illustrators, and many more in the pipeline, I can’t wait to launch the Guppy list and catapult these stories out into the world!

The Guppy website will be up and running in early 2019 but in the meantime please do get in touch if you have any queries, thoughts or ideas.

FACTS ABOUT THE GUPPY

Robert John Lechmere Guppy discovered the species in 1861.

Guppy is the common name for over 300 different species of tropical fish.

They are also known as rainbow fish or million fish.

They can be almost any colour and no two guppies are exactly alike.

They eat mosquito larvae.

They have been known to eat their own young - but no one's perfect.

Although they live in the Amazon river and can fall prey to all sorts of larger more corporate fish, guppies are nimble, individual and colourful and will always thrive…
